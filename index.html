About Me

Hello! I'm Neelesh Kumar, a Quantum Machine Learning Researcher currently pursuing my Master of Computer Applications (MCA) at Amity University Haryana (CGPA: 8.15/10). I hold a B.Sc. in Mathematics & Physics from CSJM University, Kanpur, which has provided me with a robust foundation in linear algebra, quantum mechanics, and probability theory.

My research focuses on variational quantum circuits and hybrid quantum-classical architectures for real-world applications in financial prediction and medical diagnosis. My master's thesis, supervised by Dr. Rashmi Gupta and guided by Dr. Harbinder Singh, explores hybrid quantum-classical models for financial market prediction in the NISQ era.

I am the Founder of Indian Quantum Lab, an educational platform delivering structured quantum computing curriculum to 50+ learners through hands-on Qiskit training and bootcamps. I also serve as an invited peer reviewer for Frontiers in Artificial Intelligence (2026).

Quantum Finance Predictor

Quantum Finance Predictor

2025 – 2026 · Master's Thesis, Amity University Haryana

  • Hybrid quantum-classical framework using VQC, ZZFeatureMap, and NLP-based sentiment analysis for financial market prediction.
  • Evaluated on six technology stocks, achieving 73.2% average VQC classification accuracy.
  • Benchmarked against LSTM and Random Forest using Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1-score, and MSE.
  • Accepted at ICDAM 2026, Springer LNNS.

Quantum Breast Cancer Diagnosis

Quantum-Classical Hybrid Model for Breast Cancer Diagnosis

2026 – Present · Independent Research

  • Quantum SVM (QSVM) with ZZFeatureMap, PauliFeatureMap, Angle Encoding, and custom adaptive feature maps on WDBC dataset.
  • PCA for dimensionality reduction (2–6 qubits), compared against classical SVM.
  • Benchmarking across simulator, noisy simulation (depolarizing & amplitude damping), and trapped-ion hardware.
  • Metrics: Accuracy, F1-score, ROC-AUC, paired t-tests (95% CI).
