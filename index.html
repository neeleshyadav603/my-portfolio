About Me

Hello! I'm Neelesh Kumar, an aspiring researcher in Quantum Computing & AI currently pursuing my Master of Computer Applications (MCA) at Amity University, Haryana. I hold a B.Sc. in Mathematics & Physics from CSJM Kanpur University, which has provided me with a robust analytical and theoretical foundation.

My research interests lie at the intersection of quantum computing and artificial intelligence, with a focus on developing hybrid quantum-classical models, quantum algorithms, and educational tools to democratize quantum knowledge. I am passionate about simplifying complex concepts and contributing to academic environments through research and teaching.

When I'm not exploring quantum circuits or building ML models, I enjoy creating educational content, mentoring learners through my platform Indian Quantum Lab, and staying updated with cutting-edge developments in quantum technology and AI.
